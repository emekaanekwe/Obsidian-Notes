## 🔗 Additional Pathways

If your student is enrolled in **Foundation Mathematics** or **Mathematical Methods**, the topic structure differs .

**Foundation Mathematics** focuses on:
- Practical arithmetic, percentages, and ratios
- Basic data collection and interpretation
- Financial literacy (banking, tax, budgeting)
- Measurement and geometry in real-world contexts

**Mathematical Methods** (for advanced students) includes:
- Functions and transformations (polynomials, exponentials, logarithms, circular functions)
- Differential calculus (rates of change, derivatives, applications)
- Probability and counting techniques
- Higher-level algebraic manipulation
