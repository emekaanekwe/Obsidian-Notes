### Solutions: Measures of Center and Spread

**Mean Across Two Classes:**

Total sum = $1541 + 1440 = 2981$

Total students = $23 + 20 = 43$

Combined mean = $\frac{2981}{43} \approx 69.33$

---

**10. a. ii. Original Data IQR and Standard Deviation**

IQR: measures the spread of the middle 50% resisting outlier impact
$Q_1 = \frac{5.9 + 6.0}{2} = 5.95$

$Q_3 = \frac{7.4 + 7.4}{2} = 7.4$

$IQR = 7.4 - 5.95 = 1.45$

Standard deviation:

$\sum (x - \bar{x})^2 = 6.45$

$s = \sqrt{\frac{6.45}{8}} \approx 0.90$
